Understanding The Water Problem

Morgan State University severely lacks in drinking water infrastructure. MSU deprives students of a service that is commonly found on other university campuses. Learn our motives and stand firm with our movement.

Requests and Demands

  • Purchasing Water Stations

    We want new water fountains in all significant areas of the campus.

  • 1+ Fountain Per Floor

    We should not have to travel to the next building over or take a stairway in large buildings such as the library or student center to get to water.

  • Healthy Student Living

    Students have the right to healthier lives with easy access to water.
